Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Finger Gong is an offline musical instrument. Your privacy is simple: we don't collect anything.

No Data Collection

Finger Gong does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no network connections, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

No Network Access

The app does not connect to the internet. All audio synthesis, visuals, and interactions happen locally on your device. No data leaves your device at any time.

No Third-Party Services

Finger Gong does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, analytics tools, or crash reporters.

Audio

All sounds are generated in real time using mathematical synthesis. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted. The recording/playback feature stores performance data temporarily in memory only — it is never saved to disk or sent anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Finger Gong does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
