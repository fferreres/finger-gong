Finger Gong

Download on the App Store

Every sound, built in real time

Finger Gong creates its sound the way a real gong does — through the interaction of vibrating modes. Fourteen oscillators shape each voice, detuned and coupled so harmonics bloom, beat, and slowly fade. The result is a living, breathing instrument that never plays the same way twice.

Tap for a clean strike. Hold and drag for a friction mallet rub — the gong begins to sing, harmonics building and shifting as you move. Speed, direction, and pressure all shape the voice.

See the sound

The gong's energy is visible. A 2D wave simulation ripples across the surface in real time, responding to every strike and rub. Switch to Chladni mode and watch ten thousand sand particles settle into the nodal patterns of the vibrating disc — the same physics Ernst Chladni revealed two centuries ago, alive on your screen.

Bliss mode

Curated sound environments for meditation, relaxation, and healing. Each preset tunes the gong to a specific shape, alloy, pitch, and sustain — voiced for calm. Step through the presets or stay with one that resonates. Pair with the friction mallet for slow, immersive sessions.
