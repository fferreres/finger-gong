Common Questions

No sound?
Make sure your device isn't muted (check the silent switch) and volume is up. Tap the gong surface to strike it.

How do I use friction mallets?
Select a flumie mallet, then hold and drag slowly on the gong surface. The gong will begin to sing as harmonics build.

What is Thankium?
Thankium is an exclusive fifth alloy available as an in-app purchase. It has a warm golden shimmer with longer sustain.

Can I restore purchases?
Open the alloy selector and tap Restore Purchases at the bottom.
